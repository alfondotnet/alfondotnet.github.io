Emdes Solutions LLC — operating alfon.net and NoWax Distribution

Effective Date: April 25, 2026

1. Introduction

This Consent & Privacy Notice ("Notice") describes how Emdes Solutions LLC ("Emdes Solutions," "we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, shares, and protects information in connection with the websites, products, and services we operate, including alfon.net, NoWax Distribution, and any other brands, products, or integrations we operate (collectively, the "Services").

This Notice also serves as our public privacy notice for any third-party developer platforms where it is referenced — including, without limitation, our Amazon Developer Security Profiles for Login with Amazon, the Selling Partner API, and the Amazon Advertising API.

By using the Services, or by granting us consent to access information through Login with Amazon or any Amazon API, you agree to the practices described here.

2. Scope

This Notice applies to:

  • Visitors to alfon.net and any subdomain or sibling site we operate.
  • Customers, retailers, and partners interacting with NoWax Distribution.
  • End users who choose to authenticate to one of our Services using Login with Amazon.
  • Data we receive about our own business operations through Amazon developer APIs (e.g., Selling Partner API, Advertising API) for products we distribute, including NoWax.

3. Information We Collect

The information we collect depends on how you interact with us:

  • Contact & inquiry data: name, email address, and any message content you send us (for example, wholesale or partnership inquiries).
  • Login with Amazon profile data: when you choose to sign in to one of our Services with your Amazon account, we receive only the profile information covered by the scopes you consent to — typically your name, email address, Amazon user identifier, and (where requested) postal code. We do not receive your Amazon password.
  • Amazon Selling Partner / Advertising data: for our own seller account and the products we distribute, we may access order, listing, inventory, fulfillment, financial, and advertising performance data through Amazon developer APIs. This data describes our business operations; it is not third-party customer data we resell or share.
  • Usage & device data: standard server logs, browser type, operating system, approximate (non-identifying) location derived from IP, and pages visited, used to operate and secure the Services.

We do not knowingly collect sensitive personal data such as government identifiers, health data, or payment card numbers. Payments, where applicable, are processed by third parties (e.g., Amazon, Apple) and are not accessible to us.

4. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, maintain, and improve the Services.
  • Authenticate you and create or link your account when you choose to sign in with Amazon.
  • Manage our distribution operations, including order processing, inventory, advertising performance, and customer support related to products we distribute.
  • Respond to inquiries and provide support.
  • Detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, security, and technical issues.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

Amazon-derived data limitation. Information we receive through Login with Amazon or any Amazon developer API is used only for the purpose for which you (or, where applicable, Amazon) authorized it. We do not use Amazon-derived data for unrelated marketing, profiling, or resale.

5. How We Share Information

We share information only as needed to operate the Services, and only with parties bound by appropriate confidentiality and data-protection obligations:

  • Service providers: hosting, storage, email, analytics, and similar infrastructure providers acting on our behalf.
  • Legal & safety: when required by law, regulation, valid legal process, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Emdes Solutions LLC, our users, or the public.
  • Business transfers: in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets, subject to this Notice.

We do not sell or rent personal information. We do not share Amazon-derived data with third parties except as strictly necessary to operate the Services on your behalf or as required by law.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as is necessary for the purposes described in this Notice, to comply with our legal obligations, to resolve disputes, and to enforce our agreements. Authentication tokens granted via Login with Amazon are retained only while needed to provide the Service and are deleted when you disconnect, when your session expires, or when your account is deleted.

7. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards — including TLS encryption in transit, access controls, and least-privilege practices — to protect personal information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Request a portable copy of your data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time, including by revoking our access to your Amazon account at amazon.com/ap/adam.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@alfon.net. We will respond within a reasonable time and as required by applicable law.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

10. Cookies & Similar Technologies
